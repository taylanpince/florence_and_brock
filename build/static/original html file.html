Florence and Brock: Welcome to the new website!

This website has been set up for owners and residents of Brockton Village Townhomes built by Somerset Homes in Toronto, Ontario in the summer of 2007. The owners and tenants of Brockton Village Townhomes reside in the addresses of 115 to 135 Florence Street respectively (odd numbers only). Upon construction of the 11 townhouses, the city of Toronto requested that the shared laneway at the back of the townhouses be formed into a common element condominium and be managed accordingly. This shared laneway allows owners and tenants to access their car garages as needed. On Dec 2007, the Comment Element Condominium was formed through the city Condominium Act and was designated the name Toronto Condominium Common Element Corporation # 1900 (T.C.E.C.C # 1900). In Dec 2007 a board of directors was officially elected to manage this common laneway. This website was developed by the board of directors to help facilitate the management of the laneway and provide information, financial records, audits and reports as well as allow discussion around issues that may arise due to the laneway. The main objectives of the board are to maintain clear access of the laneway in the winter months through the management of snow removal services in addition to the upkeep of the privacy fence and landscaping that lie immediately parallel to the laneway. The board of directors hope this website is helpful to all owners and tenants and we welcome your feedback on any items listed on this website.
